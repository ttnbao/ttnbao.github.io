Bio

Wireless systems research across academia and industry

Dr. Tingnan Bao is a Research Associate at the University of Ottawa. His research focuses on AI and machine learning for 6G technologies, including secure UAV-assisted communications, satellite and non-terrestrial networks, reconfigurable intelligent surfaces, and reinforcement learning for task offloading and resource allocation.

He received his Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Victoria, his M.Sc. in Information Technology from KTH Royal Institute of Technology, and his B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Liaoning Technical University. His experience spans university research, industrial research at Lenovo Research, 3GPP RAN standardization, and RAN engineering at Ericsson China.
